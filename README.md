CryptoVault is an application for securely storing your passwords on your computer. It keeps your sensitive information safe using strong encryption methods.

- **Local Storage**: CryptoVault saves your data directly on your computer, ensuring your information stays private.
- **Encryption**: Your passwords are stored securely using cryptographic techniques.
